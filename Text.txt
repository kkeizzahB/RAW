#THURSDAY MORNING PRAYER:

OUR LOVING AND CARING GOD, 

WE HUMBLE OURSELVES BEFORE YOU THIS MORNING AGAIN WITH HEARTS FULL OF THANKS GIVING. 

THANK YOU FATHER FOR BEING WITH US THROUGHOUT THE NIGHT UP TO THIS PLEASANT MORNING. 

FATHER, WE CAN'T TAKE FOR GRANTED TO BE ALIVE TODAY FOR MANY HAD SLEPT LIKE US LAST NIGHT HOPING TO SEE THIS DAY BUT THEY ARE NO LONGER THERE, NOTHING GOOD WE'VE DONE TO HAVE BEEN SPARED BUT IT'S BECAUSE OF THY GRACE AND MERCY WE'RE ALIVE. 

PLEASE HOLD US WITH THY HAND AND STAY WITH US , PROTECT US FROM SEEN AND UNSEEN DANGERS, HIDE US UNDER YOUR WINGS WHERE THE DEVIL MAY NEVER REACH US TILL THE DAY IS OVER IN JESUS' PRECIOUS NAME WE ASK ALL THESE BELIEVING AND TRUSTING. AMEN.
